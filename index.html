About The Project

Dragon Helmet is a project that aims a computer-aided life for every human on this planet. In today's world we are addicted to smart phones and our expectations from our smart phones are growing each passing day.

Everyday a new brilliant product being made by huge companies in all over the world. But none of them has a free platform or none of them is a complete solution for our need to computer in our daily lives.

As the exact opposite of these products Dragon Helmet promises for a free software and hardware platform and offers a fully computer-aided life.
